Chocolate Cake

Rich, moist, and utterly delicious

Prep Time 20 min
Cook Time 35 min
Total Time 55 min
Servings 8
Cooking Progress
0%

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, buttermilk, oil, and vanilla. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in hot water (batter will be thin). Pour into prepared pans.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then remove to wire racks to cool completely.
  7. Frost with your favorite chocolate frosting and serve!
